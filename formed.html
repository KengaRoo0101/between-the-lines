Trust layer

Data creates value only when consent and usefulness come first.

The platform should collect the minimum data needed to produce the requested output, improve product quality, and understand what users actually find valuable.

Future monetization should come from aggregated, anonymized pattern insights, premium tools, subscriptions, templates, exports, and business services—not unclear use of private raw uploads.

Monetization

Revenue should be built into the workflow, not pasted on later.

Single-use payments

Paid reports, premium exports, document packets, and one-time setup products.

Subscriptions

Saved projects, progress tracking, recurring reports, templates, and guided business workflows.

Services layer

Higher-priced support for setup, data review, launch materials, and workflow implementation.
